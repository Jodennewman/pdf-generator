Optimising for Comments

Advanced Theory and Practices ↓ Module 2: Script Writing Mastery ↓ Section 1: Optimising for Comments

Module Overview:

"Comments are currency"

The algorithm loves comments, and viral videos always have active comment sections. But great comment sections don't happen by accident - they're engineered through strategic script writing. This module reveals four proven types of comment sections and how to optimize your scripts to achieve each one.

Key Terms

FOMO
Fear Of Missing Out - creating intrigue in comments that keeps viewers watching to understand the shared experience.
REHOOK
When commenters create compelling reasons for new viewers to keep watching through their discussions.
ENGAGEMENT
Viewer interaction through comments that signals to algorithms your content is worth promoting.

Action Plan:

  • Add a small mistake viewers will want to fix
  • Save your best reveal for the last 10 seconds
  • Ask viewers to share their experiences

Key Takeaways

Strategic Mistakes & FOMO

Two key techniques that drive engagement: intentionally placed "mistakes" that viewers love to correct, and end-video hooks that create FOMO in the comments. When viewers discuss what's coming, it keeps new viewers watching longer.

  • Create correctable moments
  • End with strong hooks
  • Drive viewer discussion
  • Build shared experiences
  • Spark natural debates
  • Target diverse audiences
  • Leave strategic gaps
  • Keep authenticity

Controversy & Discussion

Controversial topics can multiply reach by engaging multiple audience segments at once. The key is subtle positioning that sparks natural discussion, rather than forcing debate. Create space for viewers to share their expertise.

Pro Tip

"Review your scripts for strategic engagement opportunities, but use these techniques sparingly. The goal is to boost occasional videos without sacrificing authenticity or community trust."

Remember...

HACK WISELY

Growth hacks can boost views, but authentic community building creates lasting success.
